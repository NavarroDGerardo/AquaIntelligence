Watch them
With this functionality you will be able to see your fish live while sitll contnuing with all your daily activities. The visor is connected through the AquaIntelligence device so it can send direct HD video to your mobile device wherever you are.
Feed them
Worried your fish might be hungry? We've got you covered. With the installed app on your phone you can feed a certain amount of food so your fish are covered in case you have to miss their usual feeding time.
Take some photos
Control you fish tank and show it to the world by taking HQ photos of it. You can either control the lights and ilumination of the tank itself and take a snap of the whole system or just some specific areas you choose.
Set the day
Planning a trip? Covering some job errand on another location? With this functionality you can actually choose how many days you might be absent, so the AquaIntelligence device can be programmed in order to feed your friends at a certain time of the day, clean the tank, regulate the water conditions, etc. The calendar can also be controlled through you device as it can be ajusted to your needs.
Clean them
Whenever your tank starts to get a little dusty from all the aquatic life happening inside of it, we can clean it with a simple click. The AquaIntelligence cleaner works with filters that constantly clean the water and mantain the walls of your tank completely transparent, while the purifying laser can be activated once a week to eliminate bacteria and other type of contaminants tht might exist within the tank.
Don't forget
While AquaIntelligence and it's devices offer a simple solution to several problems and situations, your tank is harboring a certain type of life that might be threatened by many factors. Be sure to still read and investigate about the type of fish you're getting, their life span and what other things may cause a damage. Insppropiate care of your tank may result in any life being endagered if not taken suficiently seriously. Take care of your buddies!
